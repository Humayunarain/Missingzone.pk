Short Description and Usage

Missingzone.Pk The Purpose Of This Platform Is Help To Find The Missing People Of Pakistan.

If You lost your brother, sister etc.. So You Can Easily Find Them In Our Platform If U Can Help Missed Person So You Also Include Them Information On Our Platform To Help Them.
ThankYou.


Basic Requirement

Here is the list Required To Register Any Missed Person in Missingzone.PK


  • Photo Of The Person
  • His Full Name
  • A physical description, including height, weight, age, hair color, eye color, build, etc
  • A description of the clothing and shoes the person was last seen wearing
  • A list of possessions the person might be carrying, such as jewelry, glasses, contact lenses, accessories
